Description

This hearty and satisfying dish is traditionally eaten on New Year's to bring abundance and fortune.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 7 cups water
  • 1 pound cotechino
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Pierce the cotechino with a fork in several places. In a large pot place the cotechino, chopped onion, 1 bay leaf, peppercorns and thyme.
  2. In a large pot combine the lentils, quartered onion, garlic, bay leaf, carrot and salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Remove the onion, garlic, bay leaf and carrot; discard. Spoon the lentils into a serving dish, drizzle with olive oil and slice rounds of the cotechino over the top.
